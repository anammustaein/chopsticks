Chopsticks is a hand game in which players extend a number of fingers from each hand and transfer those scores by taking turns with the computer to tap one hand against another.

Both the player and computer starts with 1 finger on each hand. On a playerâ€™s turn, they must either attack, by adding finger(s) from one of their hands to another, or split, by dividing the number of fingers between their two hands.

When a hand ends up with more than 4 fingers, then the hand is dead and can no longer be of use to the player or computer. The game ends when either the player or the computer has two dead hands.
